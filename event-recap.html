BTL Body & Mind Experience: Detroit Recap

On March 21, 2026, healthcare providers from across Michigan gathered at the Westin Book Cadillac in Detroit for a full day of clinical education, hands-on device demonstrations, and practice revenue planning.

March 21, 2026 Westin Book Cadillac, Detroit MI

Event Highlights

A full day of clinical education designed for practice owners and medical directors evaluating BTL devices.

Harvard Key Opinion Leader Keynote

The morning opened with a clinical keynote from Harvard faculty presenting published outcomes data across the BTL device portfolio. Attendees saw real clinical results, not marketing materials.

Hands-On Device Demonstrations

Every attendee had the opportunity to try each device firsthand. Clinical specialists walked providers through Emsculpt Neo, Emsella, Emface, Exion, Emtone, Emvital, and EXOMIND with live demonstrations tailored to each specialty.

Specialty Breakout Sessions

Small-group sessions organized by specialty covered clinical deep dives, patient selection criteria, and published outcomes specific to each field. Chiropractors, dermatologists, plastic surgeons, med spas, family practitioners, orthopedic surgeons, cosmetic dentists, and psychiatrists each had dedicated sessions.

Revenue Workshop & Custom ROI Modeling

The afternoon revenue workshop covered pricing strategies, staff training models, and patient marketing playbooks. Each attendee sat down with a BTL revenue specialist who built a custom financial model based on their practice's patient volume, market rates, and break-even timeline.

Evening Cocktail Hour, Dinner & DJ Party

The day wrapped with an open bar cocktail hour, sit-down dinner, and DJ party at the Westin Book Cadillac. Providers networked with peers and the BTL clinical team in a relaxed setting.

Products Showcased

Seven BTL devices were available for hands-on demonstration throughout the day.

Emsculpt Neo

Simultaneous HIFEM + synchronized RF for muscle building and fat elimination. FDA-cleared for 10 body areas. 25% more muscle, 30% less fat, 87% patient satisfaction.

Emsella

HIFEM technology for pelvic floor rehabilitation. 68% improvement in urinary incontinence at 6 months. Patients remain fully clothed during 30-minute sessions.

Emface

Synchronized RF + HIFES for facial contouring and wrinkle reduction. FDA-cleared for wrinkles, TMJ dysfunction, and submental laxity. 20-minute sessions, no needles.

Exion

The BTL multiplatform: Clear RF, Fractional RF, Face, and Body modalities. Head-to-toe coverage from a single device with smart microablation and real-time impedance monitoring.

Emtone

RF + targeted pressure energy for cellulite reduction. Four treatments, 20 minutes each. Similar to a hot stone massage with intense mechanical vibrations.

Emvital

Pain management, mobility improvement, and injury treatment targeting joints and spine. Non-pharmacological pain relief for chronic patients.

EXOMIND

Patented ExoTMS (Transcranial Magnetic Stimulation) for Major Depressive Disorder. FDA-cleared, drug-free, operator-independent sessions under 30 minutes.
